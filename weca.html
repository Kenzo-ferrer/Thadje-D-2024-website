What are some Extra Curricular Activities?

1. BADMINTON - Badminton is a dynamic racquet sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net. The game can be played in two formats: singles (one player on each side) or doubles (two players on each side). The objective is to hit a shuttlecock, also known as a birdie, over the net and into the opponent's court in a way that they cannot return it successfully

2. VOLLEYBALL - Volleyball is a team sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net, with the goal of sending a ball over the net and into the opponent’s side of the court in such a way that they cannot return it successfully. The game is typically played with two teams of six players each, though there are variations like beach volleyball with two players per team

3. BASKETBALL - Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played on a rectangular court with a hoop at each end. Each team aims to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent’s hoop, which is mounted 10 feet above the ground. Basketball combines skill, strategy, and teamwork, making it a dynamic and exciting sport enjoyed worldwide.
